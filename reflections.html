Lesson Insight Nov 2023

Adapting to Kinesthetic Learners

During the Electrical Installations module, I noticed a disconnect...

Challenge: Students struggled to translate 2D schematics into 3D physical wiring.

Action: I introduced H5P interactive videos that allowed students to "click through" a simulated circuit before touching physical wires.

Result: Assessment scores improved by 15%, and material waste decreased significantly.

Curriculum Jan 2024

Integrating Soft Skills into Hardware Labs

A reflection on bridging the gap between technical prowess and communication...

Technical proficiency is moot if the technician cannot communicate the issue. I redesigned the Computer Hardware Servicing lab to include a "Client Simulation" phase.

  • Students had to diagnose a broken PC.
  • They then had to explain the issue to a "client" (me or a peer) without using jargon.
  • Grading was 50% technical fix, 50% communication clarity.

This mirrored industry realities and improved student confidence in customer interactions.
